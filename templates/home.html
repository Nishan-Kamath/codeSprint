About Us

At Food Donation Platform, we envision a world without hunger and food waste. Our mission is to bridge the gap between surplus food and those in need, ensuring that no meal goes to waste.


"The food you waste could be on someone's plate."


Every day, over 1.3 billion tons of food is wasted globally, which is enough to feed millions of hungry people. Did you know that in India alone, approximately 68 million tons of food is wasted annually? This is equivalent to almost 40% of the food produced.


Our platform aims to bridge this gap by connecting individuals and organizations with excess food to those in need. Through technology, awareness, and community efforts, we strive to significantly reduce food wastage and ensure no one sleeps hungry.
